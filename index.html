About.

Available for Collaboration

I'm Abu Tanim — a Cybersecurity Researcher and Python Developer operating at the intersection of offensive security, network engineering, and AI-driven automation.

Pursuing my degree in Computer Science & Engineering at Premier University, Chittagong, I architect security frameworks that think adversarially — stress-testing systems before the adversaries do. My research centers on Zero Trust Architecture, where the mantra is never trust, always verify.

Beyond defensive postures, I build Python-powered automation pipelines that transform raw threat intelligence into actionable signals. Whether it's dissecting network traffic, implementing AI-augmented vulnerability scanners, or engineering CLI security toolkits, I operate with surgical precision and a bias toward clean, auditable code.
